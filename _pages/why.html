Working on a software like passbolt is quite demanding but it also does have its perks:

  • Open source: you will work within a tight-knit team, in collaboration with the broader community, shaping elegant solutions to solve challenging security problems.
  • Small team / big impact: We take collaboration at hearth. You will be part of the discussions and decisions that will shape the future of the product.
  • Attractive compensation: We offer competitive salary, employee equity. Because we are based in Luxembourg you will get access to excellent health care and services for you and your family.
  • Work/life balance: We offer 25 days of paid holidays per year. You are also invited to our bi-annual team meetings and to attend international conferences.
  • Remote work friendly: You can work up to 6 months per year wherever you would like. We will subsidies your coworking fees if you send us a postcard.
